MotorMaster is a cross-platform interface that allows for effective communication throughout the car repair process. This service gives teachers and students the time they require, but still keep clients updated daily on their car’s progress.

Teachers!
MotorMaster gives you an easy and convenient way to manage their clients and students! You can assign specific students to clients that require car repair with a simple scroll and click. The way you would assign in real life is simplified through this service. Mandatory forms, including all the automotive repair intake forms, can be filled in digitally without the hassle of mailing or faxing the form to the client. This service makes it mandatory that student updates are first checked by you before being sent to clients!

Students!
Motormaster makes updating, an easy simple process! You can fill out updates digitally without the inconvenience of bothering your teacher. These updates will be sent automatically with the click of a button! You won’t forget to update either, because there will be daily reminders to keep you on track!

Clients!
Tired of constantly visiting your car for updates? Now you can receive online updates daily to see the progress of your car’s repair! Students will send you daily updates on your car, and you will be e-mailed them automatically after teacher approval. Teachers will also send automotive intake repair forms through our website. This way you can receive all the cost and estimates in a short time, as well as complete this form digitally, and sent with the click of a button. Even better right?
